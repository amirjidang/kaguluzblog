Early Life and Education

Jessie J, born Jessica Ellen Cornish, was born on March 27, 1988, in Chadwell Heath, London, England. She had a creative upbringing, with her parents nurturing her passion for music from a young age. During her formative years, Jessie J attended Colin’s Performing Arts School, where she honed her singing and dancing skills. She later enrolled at Mayfield High School in Essex, where she continued to develop her musical abilities. Her dedication and talent were evident from a young age, and she soon caught the attention of the National Youth Music Theatre, earning a spot in their prestigious program.

After completing her secondary education, Jessie J pursued further studies at the London School of Performing Arts & Technologies.
